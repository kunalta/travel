To Visit Details Complimentaries
1 Mumbai - Dapoli Morning Dept. from Mumbai. Dapoli – Karde – Beach Relaxation.
(Enjoy Water Sports at Own Cost). Stay at Dapoli Karde. (BLD)
2 Dapoli - Ganpatipule Dabhole Chandikadevi – A popular temple situated in a cave- a holy place of Pandav Era.
Ancient Shiva Temple of Vyadeshwar is Shiv panchayatan. Hedvi Dashbhuja Ganesh built during the time of Peshwas.
Ganpatipule – Jagrut Ganesh Temple at the side of a scenic beach. Stay at Ganpatipule. (BLD)
3 Ganpatipule - Tarkarli Pawas – Samadhi of Swami Swarupanand built by his devotees.
Kunkeshwar – Jagrut Shiva temple built by an Arab with inviting sea beach at the side of Mandir.
Evening at Tarkarli Beach. Stay at Malvan / Tarkarli. (BLD)
4 Malvan - Tarkarli local sightseeing . Sindhudurga Fort built amidst the sea by Shivaji Maharaj, Tarkarli Backwater – the most inviting sea beach of Kokan.
(Snorkeling & Scuba diving: swimming underwater with special breathing equipment.
Parasailing: pulled behind a motorboat while wearing a parachute so that you sail through the air.
Watersports – Speed boat, Scooter Boat, Kayaking, Jetskiing, Bumper ride, Banana ride – Sport Activities cost not included in this package.)
Malvan : Suvarna Ganesh Mandir – Riddhi Siddhi Ganesh.
Going around Kokani mewa at Malvan bazar ( Cashewnut, Kokam, Khadekade Ladu). Stay at Sawantwadi. (BLD)
5 Malvan - Sawantwadi Sawantwadi – Wooden Colourful Toys Market. Amboli Hill Station – Hiranykeshi Mandir, Amboli Waterfall.
Kolhapur – Mahalaxmi Devi Darshan. Night Departure for Mumbai. (BLD)
6 Mumbai Arrival Early Morning arrival at Mumbai.
