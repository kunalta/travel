OVERVIEW

Maharashtra Wildlife Tour is a well-planned itinerary by Indian Holiday that offers one a chance to spot a vast variety of wildlife species of the region at two of the best wildlife reserves of the state- Tadoba National Park and Nagzira Wildlife Sanctuary. During the itinerary of 4 nights and 5 days, embark on wildlife safaris inside both these wildlife reserves and spot the big cats and other animal and bird species. Experience the thrill of watching the animals roaming around freely in their natural habitats.

Note: This is just a suggested itinerary indicative of what could be possible. We tailor holidays for your specific needs. Contact us if you want modifications so that we could tailor a holiday to suit your need for an unforgettable India tour.

TRIP HIGHLIGHTS

  • Wildlife safari at Tadoba National Park
  • Wildlife spotting at Nagzira Wildlife Sanctuary

ITERNARARY

After arriving at the Nagpur railway station / Airport, meet an IHPL representative who helps you with an assisted transfer to Tadoba National Park. Upon reaching the park, complete the check-in formalities. Post the formalities, you are free to spend the day at leisure. Enjoy the facilities of the resort, enjoy a leisure walk and so on. Popular as the ‘Jewel of Vidharba’, Tadoba National Park is home to a vast and rich variety of flora and fauna. It is also a Tiger Reserve and houses a decent population of the big cats. Stay Overnight.


Day 2, post an early breakfast, is scheduled for a wildlife safari inside the national park. During the safari, some of the species that you would spot here in abundance include jackals, sloth bears, tigers, hyenas, panthers, nil gai, bison, sambar, wild dogs and cheetal. The oldest and largest national park of Maharashtra, Tadoba is also home to around 195 avifauna and 74 butterfly species. Spend a thrilling day here. Overnight stay at the resort.


After breakfast, get transferred to Nagzira Wildlife Sanctuary, by road. After reaching, check-in at the wildlife resort. This wildlife sanctuary is home to around 4 amphibian species, 34 mammal species, 36 reptile species, and 166 species of birds. It receives around 30,000 visitors every year. The rest of the day is at leisure. One of the nearby attractions is Itiadoh dam (65 km), which is worth-visiting. This dam is built in Garvi River. If you wish you can embark on a wildlife safari inside the park, late afternoon. Stay Overnight.


After an early morning breakfast, embark on morning wildlife safari inside the park. Spot animals roaming freely. Some of the animals and birds that can normally be seen here include sloth bears, panthers, leopards, chital, tiger, bisons, jackals, mouse deers, jungle cat, hare, barking deer, civet cats, four-headed antelopes, grey jungle fowl, peafowl and more. Later in the day, get transferred to Nagpur, by road. Complete the check-in formalities. The evening is at leisure. You can explore the nearby surroundings on-foot, visit the popular attractions, shop in the popular markets, and taste the local delicacies and more. Stay Overnight.


Post breakfast, complete the check-out formalities and get transferred to Nagpur airport/railway station to board a flight or a train for your onward destination.
