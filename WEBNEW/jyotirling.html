Tour Detail

To Visit Details Complimentaries
1 Mumbai - Bhimashankar - Ahmednagar

Early morning proceed from Mumbai to Bhimashankar . Visit Bhimashankar an ancient shrine situated in
the Sahyadri hills . It is one of the well known jyotirlinga amongst the 12 jyotirlingas.
After Darshan proceed to Ahmednagar. Arrive at Ahmednagar in the evening.
2 Ahmednagar - Parali Vaijnath - Ambajogai

Early morning proceed to Ambajogai - a heritage temple dedicated to Goddess Yogeshwari.br Later visit Parali Vaijnath Jyotirling temple. Arrive at Ambajogai in the evening.
3 Ambajogai - Aundha Nagnath - Aurangabad Proceed to Aurangabad.
Enroute visit Aundha Naganath Jyotirling Temple. Arrive at Aurangabad in the evening.
4 Aurangabad Aurangabad city tour includes Grishneshwar Temple- one of the joytirlings,
Later visit Ellora caves, Bhadra Maruti temple - the only sleeping Maruti idol in India, Pawanchakki and Bibi ka Maqbara.
4 Aurangabad - Shirdi Proceed to Shirdi. Evening visit Sai Baba Temple.
5 Departure from Shirdi Proceed to Mumbai. Enroute visit Trimbakeshwar - one of the 12 Jyotirlings. Tour concludes.
